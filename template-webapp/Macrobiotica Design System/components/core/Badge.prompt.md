Small status pill; soft tone background with matching text. Use chakra tones for energetic categories, semantic tones for state.

```jsx
<Badge tone="green">On track</Badge>
<Badge tone="heart" dot>Balanced</Badge>
<Badge tone="warning">More yang</Badge>
```

- `tone`: `neutral` | `green` | `success` | `warning` | `danger` | `info` | `root` | `sacral` | `solar` | `heart` | `throat` | `third-eye` | `crown`
- `dot`: leading dot in tone color
